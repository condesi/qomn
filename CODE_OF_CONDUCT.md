# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in the QOMN project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of experience level, gender identity, orientation, disability, appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, age, religion, or nationality.

## Expected behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Be respectful of differing viewpoints.
- Gracefully accept constructive criticism.
- Focus on what is best for the project and the broader community of engineers, researchers, students, and practitioners who may use this work.
- Show empathy toward other community members.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment of any kind, public or private.
- Personal attacks on contributors or maintainers.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Off-topic political or ideological discussions. This repository is a working technical artifact and discussions must stay on the technical content.
- Trolling, insulting comments, or deliberate disruption.

## Enforcement

The project maintainer is responsible for clarifying standards of acceptable behavior and will take corrective action in response to any violation. Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to `percy.rojas@condesi.pe`. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated; the reporter's identity will remain confidential.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within project spaces (issues, pull requests, commit messages, discussions) and in public spaces when an individual is representing the project.
